welcome
The typewriter that you see here is the Remington Rand Deluxe Model 5, a model manufactured in the 1940's. I was inspired to acquire a typewriter after reading about Tom Hanks' quirky fascination and passion for these mechanical word factories.
My relationship with technology is a necessary one, though one which continues to exist with a faint whisper of the hackneyed, hyphenated adjective, "love-hate."
You are welcome to enter and peer about into the world of the man who sits on the proverbial fence regarding his relationship to the aforementioned modern necessity.
